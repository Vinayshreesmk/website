BOUNTY

A bounty page is a webpage dedicated to offering rewards for completing specific tasks or achieving certain goals. The content of a bounty page typically includes a clear description of the task or goal, the amount of the reward offered, and any requirements or guidelines for completing the task. Additionally, the bounty page may include information about the sponsoring organization or individual, the deadline for completing the task, and any rules or regulations governing the competition.
