What is the Strapi Community Organization?

The Strapi Community Organization is an independent, decentralized community in partnership with outstanding community members who provide value to Strapi users by creating well-maintained plugins, integrations, and tooling.

The Story of Strapi

We believe in a future in which everybody has the power to create content, digital products and user experiences that impact the world.

This dedicated GitHub organization already includes popular repositories such as the awesome-strapi repository, Dockerize, to add docker support for a Strapi Project or strapi-plugin-rest-cache.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a flexible, customizable, and scalable platform that enables developers to build content-rich applications and websites. We want to empower developers to create exceptional digital experiences for their users by giving them the tools they need to manage content efficiently.

Our Vision

We believe in a world where technology unleashes content creativity and productivity for all. In the mid-term, our main priority must be the construction of an excellent CMS. As we move forward, we will have the opportunity to extend our product. This will be done by internalizing some services we are integrated with. That way, our users will have a seamless experience.

Our Values

Strapi is made of passionate, truthful and caring team members. People who can question themselves and change their minds, while being creative, learners at heart, laser-focused, and leading by example.

Welcome to the Strapi Community

Strapi is an open-source headless CMS that allows you to build APIs quickly and easily. But it's not just the software that makes Strapi great - it's also the community of users and developers who are passionate about building amazing digital experiences.

There are many ways to get involved with the Strapi community, including:

  • Joining the Strapi forum to ask questions and share your knowledge with others
  • Contributing to the Strapi Github repository to help improve the software
  • Participating in local Strapi meetups or events to connect with other Strapi enthusiasts
  • Following Strapi on social media to stay up-to-date on the latest news and updates

By joining the Strapi community, you can:

  • Get help and support from other users and developers
  • Learn new tips and tricks for building APIs with Strapi
  • Contribute to the open-source community and make a difference in the world of web development
  • Network with other professionals in your field

Don't miss out on the opportunity to be part of this vibrant and passionate community. Join today!
